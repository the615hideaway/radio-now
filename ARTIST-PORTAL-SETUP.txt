ARTIST PORTAL SETUP
===================

Gives invited artists a dashboard: download counts, activity history,
and DJ contact email when the DJ opted to share it.

1. REDEPLOY APPS SCRIPT
-----------------------
Open your Radio Now Google Sheet → Extensions → Apps Script
Paste the latest google-apps-script/Code.gs
Deploy → Manage deployments → Edit → New version → Deploy

New endpoints: artist_login, artist_activate, artist_dashboard

2. ARTIST ACCOUNTS SHEET (auto-created)
---------------------------------------
Tab name: Artist Accounts

Columns:
  artist_account_id | artist_name | email | password_hash | password_salt | status | created_at

artist_name MUST match the catalog "Artist Name" in Sheet1 exactly
(case-insensitive). Example: David Parmley

3. INVITE AN ARTIST (you do this in the sheet)
----------------------------------------------
Add a row:

  artist_account_id: art-xxxxxxxx  (any unique id, e.g. art-david01)
  artist_name:       David Parmley  (must match catalog)
  email:             artist@example.com
  password_hash:     (leave blank)
  password_salt:     (leave blank)
  status:            invited
  created_at:        2026-06-13

Tell the artist:
  1. Go to …/artist-dashboard.html
  2. Click "Activate Invite"
  3. Enter the invited email + choose a password

4. ARTIST SIGN-IN (after activate)
----------------------------------
Sign In tab with email + password.

5. WHAT ARTISTS SEE
-------------------
- Total / week / month download counts for their songs
- Charts for their songs only
- Each download: song, DJ name/station/show, format
- DJ email only if that DJ turned on "share my email with artists"

6. TEST WITH SAMMY'S DOWNLOADS
------------------------------
Invite row for David Parmley with your email, activate, dashboard
should show Sammy's logged downloads for David Parmley songs.

7. NO PUBLIC SIGNUP
-------------------
Artists cannot self-register. Only rows with status=invited and no
password can use Activate Invite.
